The Face with Naomi Campbell

Season 2 premiered March 5, 2014 on Oxygen.

The Face was an American modeling competition on Oxygen in which supermodel coaches led teams of aspiring models through real-world fashion challenges. Season 2 featured Naomi Campbell, Anne V., and Lydia Hearst as coaches, with contestants competing to become the face of Frédéric Fekkai’s hair care brand.
