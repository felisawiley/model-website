Aspen Sojourner

“Patriot Games” · Midwinter/Spring 2017 issue · aspensojo.com

Aspen Sojourner is a lifestyle magazine covering Aspen and the Roaring Fork Valley. This editorial, “Patriot Games,” paired fall/winter 2016 designer fashion with Rococo-inspired wigs and antique set pieces. Photographed by Bernhard Donaubauer; art direction by Anne Reeser.
