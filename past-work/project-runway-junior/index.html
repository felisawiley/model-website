Project Runway Junior

Season 2 premiered December 22, 2016 on Lifetime.

Project Runway Junior was a Lifetime spin-off of Project Runway featuring fashion designers ages 13 to 17. Season 2 aired from December 2016 through February 2017, hosted by Tim Gunn with judges including Kelly Osbourne and Christian Siriano. FEE modeled for finalist Isabella “Izzy” Li-Kostrzewa, who finished in fourth place.
