About

FEE is a professional model based in Philadelphia by way of St. Thomas, USVI. She began her modeling career with Next Model Management, working across New York, Los Angeles, and Miami with clients including Charlotte Tilbury and IWC Schaffhausen, walking for designers like LaQuan Smith, and appearing on Oxygen’s The Face, mentored by Naomi Campbell, and Project Runway Junior.

Her work includes editorial, beauty, lifestyle, and commercial.
